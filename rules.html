General Information

We, the entire Zenith Staff Team, want to make sure that everyone on our Discord server and Minecraft server has a safe and fair experience. To achieve this, we have to set strict rules to which everyone has to adhere to.

Breaking the rules will result in a (temporary) loss of the ability to chat or even speak. A lack of knowledge about the rules won't save you from punishment and will be treated as a mistake from your side.
